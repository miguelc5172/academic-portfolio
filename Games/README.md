## P.E.M.D.A.S.: Training Day
P.E.M.D.A.S.: Training Day is an educational game that teachers players how to use order of operations to solve math problems. You play as a new recruit for the Problem Eradicating Math Defense Assault Squad (PEMDAS) tasked with eradicating the world of monsters through the power of math. This game focuses on your first day in which you undergo a training simulation utilizing the Advanced Mathematical Combat System (AMCS) which helps target enemy weakpoints as well as defend from enemy attacks.
